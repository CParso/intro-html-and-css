This is a profile of Gudetama the lazy egg.

Gudetama is a cartoon character from 2013 produced by the Japanese company Sanrio. The character is an anthropomorphized egg yolk whose main traits are laziness and sadness. The name "Gudetama" is derived from the Japanese words for lazy and egg.

Additionally, there are many other depictions of Gudetama corresponding to the various ways of cooking eggs. Gudetama commonly appears as:

  • an unpeeled egg
  • scrambled eggs
  • a fried egg
  • a boiled egg
  • a sliced egg
  • an egg salad sandwich
  • eggs benedict
  • custard
  • mayonnaise
  • a century egg
  • pastries made with egg
